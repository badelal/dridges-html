DRIDGES PRIVACY POLICY

UPDATES ON JUNE 1ST 2021

Dridges is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Notice informs you how Dridges collects, uses, secures and shares your information collected by Dridges when you interact with Dridges in the following ways: Please note that Dridges website may include links to other organizations’ websites whose privacy practices may differ from those of Dridges. If you submit Personal Data to any of those sites, your information is governed by their privacy notices. Dridges encourage you to carefully read the privacy notices of any website you visit.

1. Changes to Our Privacy Notice
This Privacy Notice may change from time to time.
If Dridges make a change to this Privacy Notice that Dridges believe materially affects how Dridges process your Personal Data, Dridges will provide notice of such change on Dridges website. By continuing your Interactions after those changes become effective, you accept the provisions of Dridges revised Privacy Notice.
In addition, Dridges may provide you with “just-in-time” disclosures or additional information about the data collection, use and sharing practices. These notices may provide more information about Dridges privacy practices, or provide you with additional choices about how Dridges process your Personal Data. The notices and information are subject to this Privacy Notice unless specifically indicated otherwise in the notice.

2. The Information We Collect and Store
Except as described in this Privacy Notice, Dridges will not give, sell, rent or loan any Personal Data to any third party. Dridges may collect and process the following Personal Data in connection with Dridges Interactions with you:
Personal Data You Voluntarily Provide.
Dridges will collect Personal Data when you voluntarily provide it to Dridges (including to Dridges service providers or other parties who collect it on Dridges behalf). For example, Dridges collect Personal Data when you request information about Dridges Service, subscribe to marketing communications, complete surveys, or sign up for a Dridges event or webinar. Dridges may also collect Personal Data from you offline, such as when you attend one of Dridges events, or during phone calls with sales representatives. The Personal Data Dridges collect may include contact information (such as your name, address, telephone number or email address), professional information (such as your job title, department or job role), and contact preferences. Dridges also collect information you choose to provide to Dridges when requesting information or completing any “free text: boxes in Dridges forms (for example, for event sign-up), or the nature of your request or communication.
Log Data
When you use the Service, Dridges automatically record information from your device, its software, and your activity using the Service (“Log Data”). This may include the device’s Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser type, the web page visited before you came to Dridges website, information you search for on Dridges website, locale preferences, identification numbers associated with your devices, your mobile carrier, date and time stamps associated with transactions, system configuration information, metadata concerning your Files, and other interactions with the Service. Dridges may use your IP address to identify the general geographic area from which you are accessing the website. While an IP address may reveal your ISP or geographic area, Dridges cannot determine your identity solely based upon your IP address.
Location Data
Dridges may collect geolocation and proximity of your device if location services are enabled on your device (e.g., GPS-based functionality on mobile devices used to access Dridges website), and may use that information to customize the Interactions with location-based information and features. If you access Dridges website through a mobile device and you do not want your device to provide Dridges with location-tracking information, you can disable the GPS or other location-tracking functions on your device, provided your device allows you to do this.
Cookies
Dridges use “cookies” to collect information and improve Dridges Interactions with you. A cookie is a small data file that Dridges transfer to your device. Dridges may use “persistent cookies” to save your registration ID and login password for future logins to Dridges website, such as, to register for webinars. Dridges may use “session ID cookies” to enable certain features of the Service, to better understand how your Interactions with Dridges and to monitor aggregate usage and web traffic routing on Dridges website. For more information on how Dridges use Cookies and similar technologies, please see Dridges Cookie Policy.

3. Third Party Sources of Information
You may choose to register events sponsored by Dridges partners where Dridges will share your information with Dridges marketing partners. Dridges will provide notice of the intent to share your data with Dridges partners at the point of personal data collection. To engage in marketing activities, Dridges may collect Personal Data disclosed by you on message boards, chat features, blogs and other services or platforms to which you are able to post information and materials (including third party services and platforms). Dridges process such information to better understand you, to maintain and improve the accuracy of the information Dridges store about you, and to better promote or optimize Dridges Interactions with you, including providing information about Dridges Service.

4. How We Use Personal Data
Your Personal Data may be used for the following purposes:
a) Respond to Requests.
Dridges may process your Personal Data when you contact us, such as with questions, concerns, feedback, disputes or issues, to respond to your request or provide information requested by you. Dridges share this information with third parties upon your request, or Dridges service providers or partners to the extent necessary to facilitate the Interactions. Without your Personal Data, Dridges cannot respond to you or ensure your continued use and enjoyment of the website.
b) Ensure the Security of the Service.
Dridges is committed to ensuring your safety and continued enjoyment of Dridges website. To do so, Dridges may process your Personal Data to help monitor, prevent and detect fraud, enhance security, monitor and verify identity or access, and combat spam or other malware or security risks. combat spam, malware, malicious activities or security risks; improve and enforce Dridges security measures; and to monitor the security of Dridges website.
c) Facilitate and Evaluate Use of the Online Properties.
Dridges may use your Personal Data to provide you with the online services, to facilitate your use of the online services (such as facilitating navigation and the login process, preserving information between sessions and enhancing security), to improve quality, to evaluate page response rates and determine content.
d) Improve the Accuracy of Dridges Records.
Dridges may use the Personal Data Dridges receive from you or third parties to better understand you and/or maintain and improve the accuracy of the records Dridges hold about you.
e) Post Testimonials.
Dridges may use Personal Data to post testimonials on Dridges website. Prior to posting a testimonial, Dridges will obtain your consent to use your name and testimonial. You can request your testimonial be updated or deleted at any time by sending a request with your name, testimonial location and contact information.
f) Personalize your Experience.
Dridges may provide you plugins, and other options to personalize your experience. Dridges also may use cookies and similar tracking technology for such purposes – please see Dridges Cookie Policy for more information. By personalizing Dridges Interactions, it enables to more easily use Dridges website because Dridges keep track of your preferences (e.g. your language selection, your time zone, etc.). Without processing your Personal Data for such purposes, you may not be able to access or personalize part or all of Dridges Interactions.
g) Marketing:
Dridges may use information Dridges obtain from you, your interactions with Dridges and Dridges website, and from third party sources to provide you with marketing and promotional communications, to deliver targeted and relevant advertising and marketing to you, to determine the effectiveness of Dridges marketing and promotional campaigns, to better understand you and your preferences, and to position and promote Dridges Services. Dridges marketing will be conducted in accordance with your advertising / marketing preferences and as permitted by applicable law.
h) Quality Control and Training.
Dridges may use or access Personal Data for the purposes of quality control related to the website and staff training.
i) Conferences and Events.
Dridges and Dridges partners may use Personal Data to communicate with you about Dridges events or Dridges partner events. After the event, Dridges may contact you about the event and related products and services, and may share information about your attendance with your company and Dridges conference sponsors and partners, where legally permitted to do so. If a partner or conference sponsor directly requests your Personal Data at their conference booths or presentations, your Personal Data will be handled in accordance with their privacy practices. Dridges recommend that you review the privacy practices of such partners and sponsors.
j) Provide Online Communities and Blogs:
Dridges may use Personal Data and other information disclosed by you on Dridges message boards, chat features, blogs and other services or platforms to which you are able to post information and materials for the purposes of providing you and Dridges customers with a forum to discuss Dridges Services, responding to your request, providing you with support, improving Dridges website or any other purposes set forth in this Privacy Notice. Any information that is disclosed in those forums becomes public information and may therefore appear in public ways, such as through search engines or other publicly available platforms, and may be “crawled” or searched by third parties. It could also be read, collected or used by other users to send you unsolicited messages. Please do not post any information that you do not want to reveal to the public at large.
k) Other Legitimate Business Purposes.
Dridges may use your Personal Data when it is necessary for other legitimate purposes such as protecting Dridges confidential and proprietary information. If, in the future, Dridges use your Personal Data in any way that is not described in this Privacy Notice, Dridges will disclose this to you. At that time, you can choose not to allow Dridges to use your Personal Data for any purpose that is incompatible with the purposes for which Dridges originally collected it or subsequently obtained your consent. If you choose to limit the ways Dridges can use your Personal Data, some or all of the website may not be available to you, and Dridges may not be able to interact with you.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure
Dridges only disclose your Personal Data to those who really need access to perform their tasks and third parties who have a legitimate purpose for accessing it. Dridges may share Personal Data with third parties in the following situations:
a) Service Providers, Business Partners and Others.
 Dridges may share your Personal Data with third parties, such as vendors, consultants, agents and other service providers who work on Dridges behalf. Examples include vendors and service providers who provide assistance with marketing, billing, processing credit card payments, data analysis and insight, technical support and customer service. Unless described in this Privacy Notice, Dridges do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your Personal Data with third parties for their own promotional purposes.
b) Compliance with Laws and Law Enforcement Requests;
Protection of Dridges Rights.Dridges may use or disclose Personal Data stored in your Dridges account and other Personal Data about you to third parties when Dridges have a good faith belief that disclosure is reasonably necessary to:
(i) comply with a law, regulation or legal requests including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements; (ii) protect the safety of any person from death or serious bodily injury;
(iii) prevent fraud or abuse of Dridges or its user, or
(iv) protect Dridges property rights.
c) Vital Interests: Dridges may disclose information when Dridges believe it necessary to protect the vital interests of any person.
d) Business Transfers. If Dridges is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of Dridges assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, but Dridges will notify you (for example, via email and/or a prominent notice on Dridges website) of any change in control or use of your Personal Data, or if either become subject to a different Privacy Notice. Dridges will also notify you of choices you may have regarding the information.
e) With Your Consent: Dridges may disclose your Personal Data for any purpose with your consent.

6. Marketing Opt Out and Choice
 If you sign up to receive marketing or informational announcements from us, such emails will include the capability to opt-out of receiving such e-mails in the future. Marketing and informational announcements include any communications to you that are only based on advertising or promoting products and services. Transactional communications about an Interaction, or Dridges Services are not considered “marketing” or “informational” communications.

7. Transfer of Personal Data
 If you Interact with Dridges or provide your Personal Data to us, your Personal Data may be transferred to, processed and maintained on servers or databases located outside of the country or jurisdiction where you are located. Such countries or jurisdictions may have data protection laws that are less protective than the laws of the jurisdiction in which you reside. If you do not want your Personal Data transferred to or processed or maintained outside of the country or jurisdiction where you are located, you should not use this website, Dridges resources, or the Services. If you are located in the European Economic Area (“EEA”), the United Kingdom or Switzerland, Dridges comply with applicable laws to provide an adequate level of data protection for the transfer of your Personal Data to the US. Dridges is certified under the EU-U.S. and the Swiss-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework and adheres to the Privacy Shield Principles. For more, see Section 13 below. Where applicable law requires Dridges to ensure that an international data transfer is governed by a data transfer mechanism, Dridges will use an appropriate lawful transfer mechanism.

8. Security
 The security of your Personal Data is important to us. Dridges employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security that is appropriate for Dridges Interactions with you. Dridges follow generally accepted standards to protect the Personal Data submitted to us, both during transmission and once Dridges receive it. No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, however. Therefore, Dridges cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on Dridges website, you can contact Dridges at info@dridges.com

9. Accessing or Updating Your Personal Data

If you would like to correct or update the Personal Data you provided to Dridges in relation to Dridges Interactions with you or in connection with Dridges Service, please contact info@Dridges.com Dridges will use responsible efforts to respond to your inquiry as soon as practicable. When updating your Personal Data, Dridges may ask you to verify your identity before Dridges can act on your requests. Individuals located in the European Economic Area, Switzerland, or the United Kingdom during data collection, or that are California residents should refer to the relevant sections below for more information about their access, correction and other rights in relation to their Personal Data.

10. Data Retention
Dridges may retain and use your Personal Data as necessary to comply with Dridges legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Consistent with these requirements, Dridges will try to delete your Personal Data within the time limits imposed by applicable law, if any upon request. When Dridges have no justifiable business need to process your Personal Data, Dridges will either delete or anonymize it, or, if this is not possible (for example, because your Personal Data has been stored in backup archives), then Dridges will securely store your Personal Data and isolate it from any further processing until deletion is possible. While retention requirements can vary by country, Dridges generally apply the retention periods noted below.
Marketing.
Dridges store Personal Data used for marketing purposes indefinitely until you unsubscribe. Once you unsubscribe from marketing communications, Dridges add your contact information to Dridges suppression list to ensure Dridges respect your unsubscribe request.
Web Behaviour Data.
Dridges retain any information collected via cookies, clear gifs, flash cookies, webpage counters and other technical or analytics tools up to one year from expiry of the cookie or the date of collection. Please refer to Dridges Cookie Policy for more information.
Telephone Records.
As required by applicable law, Dridges will inform you that a call will be recorded before doing so. Any telephone calls with you may be kept for a period of up to six years.

11. Our Policy Toward Children
 Our website and Services are not directed to persons under 18. Dridges do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children under 18. Any individuals under the age of 18 must have consent to Interact with Dridges or use the Services from their parent or guardian. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided Dridges with Personal Data without their consent, he or she should contact Dridges at info@dridges.com If Dridges become aware that a child under 18 has provided Dridges with Personal Data, Dridges will take steps to delete such information from Dridges files.

12. Privacy Rights
 A business subject to Civil Code section 1798.83 is required to disclose to its customers, upon request, the identity of any third parties to whom the business has disclosed Personal Data information within the previous calendar year, along with the type of Personal Data disclosed, for the third parties’ direct marketing purposes. Please note that under law, businesses are only required to respond to a customer request once during any calendar year.
