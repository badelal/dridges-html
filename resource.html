1. ABOUT DRIDGES

Dridges applicant tracking systems (ATS) evolved to help employers and recruiters take advantage of AI driven technology in hiring to accurately hire with speed. We help our clients transform into the new age of digital recruitment Like enterprise software's turned into the cloud based platforms, the ATS also evolved with time. Today, we have a wide range of ATS software options, some that concentrate on automating as much as possible of the hiring process and others that help the hiring manager to make their own decisions. The ATS has become an integral part of every aspect of hiring from building an employer brand and smarter application forms, to resume parsing, automatic retrieval of candidates’ public profiles, and scheduling interviews. Recruiting analytics and reporting tools can now help companies get a clearer understanding of the hiring process and make improvements that have a real impact on the business. The purpose of this ATS guide is to help you understand your needs as clearly as possible in order to make a choice of recruiting software.
