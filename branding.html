BRANDING AND SOURCING

MASTERING YOUR EMPLOYER BRAND TO FUEL TALENT ACQUISITION
DEFINING EMPLOYER BRANDING

ATTRACT AND SOURCE

Online CandidBranding your organization is a key to sourcing top talent in the marketplace. Your employer brand tells the world, including your active and passive candidates, what it’s like to work for your company. From the day to day operations, to team activities, to community. You can brand literally everything that encompasses your business is correlated to your employer brand. And in today’s dynamic market, to get your “step up” on the search for talent, your branding is crucial to the success of your talent acquisition strategy. Branding is where the talent attraction or sourcing process within the candidate journey begins.

Corporate Brand & Employer Brand Your corporate brand and your employer brand are connected but distinct. When a potential source connects with your website or a candidate engages with you on social media, you as an organization are telling them a story. These omni channel supported avenues become channels to educate and influence your target audience. Some candidates will be attracted to your company on the basis of your corporate brand, just as some consumers will be attracted to your products on the basis of your employer brand.

Generally speaking, however, your ability to source and engage candidates and secure top talent is dependent on the strength of your employer brand alone. Creating an employer brand keeps candidates both passive and active informed and excited about what your company stands for and what your company delivers to its employees and community. Sharing targeted, applicable and welcomed content allows these candidates to organically learn about your brand as a stand out entity and start the process of understanding what your brand has to offer when it comes to employment opportunities.
